A little bit about me

Hi, I’m Perry, an enthusiastic and self-motivated front end web developer from Kent. I currently perform front end magic for leading scientific and medical information publisher Nature Publishing Group.

I have several years of commercial experience in HTML(5), CSS(3), LESS, Sass, XSLT, XML, jQuery, Javascript, Photoshop, GitHub and Agile. I'm renowned for fast HTML and CSS coding, accuracy and attention to detail as well as awesome interpersonal skills and a relaxed temperament.

I've sprinkled my front end magic dust for startups BraveNewTalent, BoughtByMany and 300notes as well as MyTrainTicket and digital agency Eonic in addition to various sites as a freelancer.

When I’m not coding I can usually be found on my beloved mountain bike in a multitude of muddy, wet locations trying very hard not to fall off (with only a moderate success rate).
