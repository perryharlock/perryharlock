A little bit about me

Hi, I’m Perry, an enthusiastic and self-motivated UI developer / designer from Kent. I spend my days on UI design for web and apps as well as front end web builds. Recent fun includes the building of a new landing page for Cloak. Before this I built and helped with the design of our company website www.assertis.co.uk. I was also responsible for the production design of the new Gatwick Express App.

I am part of the team that made the Train Ticket Purchasing system for ThamesLink, Southern, Southeastern, Gatwick Express and other major train operators.

I have several years of commercial experience in HTML(5), CSS(3), LESS, jQuery, Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, GitHub and Agile. I'm renowned for fast HTML and CSS coding, accuracy and attention to detail as well as awesome interpersonal skills and a relaxed temperament.

I've also worked at Hire Space, Nature Publishing Group, BraveNewTalent and BoughtByMany as well as digital agency Eonic in addition to various sites as a freelancer.

When I’m not coding I can usually be found on my beloved mountain bike in a variety of muddy, wet locations trying hard not to fall off (with moderate success).
